THE FC 26 ePG SEASON 3 TOURNAMENT IS OUT!!! 🔥🔥

The tournament is a one-day event, kicking off at 9 AM prompt. In Amos ONIL'S Game Center beside Ay-Pizza upstairs, located in Jaja Complex opposite King Jaja Hall, UNILAG. A UCL format, where players must perform well in the group stage to advance. Winning matches in the knockout stage is crucial for progression. The ultimate goal is to win the Final and claim the championship title.

Tournament Format

  • Format: UCL format with players divided into groups.
  • Fixtures: Filled via blind draw.
  • Game: All Games are played on PS5, EA Sports FC 26 (no coaching allowed).

Player Guidelines

  • Controllers: Supplied by ePG officials; players may use their own but must be familiar with their functions.
  • Team Selection: Regular season teams only; no all-star teams.
  • Conduct: Slurs or damaging equipment will result in instant disqualification.
  • Attendance: Players must be present 20 minutes before matches. Lateness results in forfeiture and automatic bye for the opponent.

Match Settings

  • Halves: 5 minutes each.
  • Difficulty: World Class
  • Injuries: On
  • Offside: On
  • Time/Score Display: On
  • Camera: Co-op
  • Radar: 2D or 3D

Overtime Rules

If tied at the end of regular time, matches proceed to extra time and then penalties if still tied.

Disputes and Appeals

Participation constitutes full agreement to these rules. ePG officials' decisions are final.

For disputes, submit a formal appeal in writing to thepeopleconnect1@gmail.com.

Tournament Structure

32 players divided into 8 groups of 4 players.

Goal: Be the last player standing.

Group Stage

Each player competes against every other player in their group.

Top 2 from each group (16 total) advance to the knockout stage.

Knockout Stage

Round of 16 → Quarter-Finals → Semi-Finals → Final.

Winners progress until the Champion is crowned.
